Characters In Noli Me Tangere


Picture: Name: Description:
Crisostomo Ibarra His full name is Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin. He is the son of Don Rafael. He studied in Spain and didn't go home for 7 years. He is tall and you can see from his face that he is part Spanish. He was popular at that time because he came from another country and a lot of people were interested and questioned him.
Rafael Ibarra He is Crisostomo's dad. He was charged with the death of an "Artilyero". He was even called "erehe at pilibustero" (meaning: stubborn and rebel). Unfortunately, he was arreasted and because of that he commited suicide due to him feeling betrayed.
Padre Damaso He is a priest in the place, San Diego. In the story, he had a misunderstanding with Crisostomo and Padre Sibyla. He was also easily to get mad and he thinks that he is higher than most people in the party.
Padre Sibyla He is a priest from Binondo. In the story, he had a little fight with Padre Damaso but won because he got the seating he wanted.
Tinyenteng Guevarra He helped Don Rafael to find a lawyer. He is a friend of Don Rafael's. In the story, he told Crisostomo the whole truth about his father's death.
Kapitan Tiago His full name is Don Santiago de los Santos. He is the host of the party that was hosted. He is known for his grand house near the lake.
